## Packages
framer-motion | Smooth animations for page transitions and micro-interactions
date-fns | formatting dates for events and posts
clsx | utility for constructing className strings conditionally
tailwind-merge | utility for merging tailwind classes without conflicts
react-day-picker | Calendar component for event selection

## Notes
- Auth is handled via generic OAuth (Replit Auth blueprint)
- Images are handled via URL strings (presumed external hosting or Unsplash for demo)
- Mobile-first design approach
- "Stories" are posts with type='story' and will be displayed horizontally
